Valkyrie Chariot

This battle suit is known for its high mobility and agility in combat. Equipped with advanced thrusters and maneuvering systems, the Valkyrie Chariot can swiftly dart around the battlefield, dodging attacks and closing in on enemies with ease. Its primary focus is on hit-and-run tactics, utilizing rapid strikes and evasive maneuvers to overwhelm opponents.



















 Snowy Sniper

As the name suggests, this battle suit specializes in long-range sniping and precision shooting. It also possesses abilities to control the battlefield with freezing attacks, slowing down foes and creating opportunities for precise strikes.



















 Yamabuki Armor

Yamabuki Armor is a defensive-oriented battle suit designed to withstand heavy punishment on the front lines. Its durable armor plating and shield generators provide excellent protection for both itself and nearby allies. In addition to its defensive capabilities, Yamabuki Armor can also deploy support drones and energy barriers to bolster its team's defenses and provide tactical advantages.



















 Drive Kometa
